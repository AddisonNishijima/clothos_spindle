WHAT WE DO

Clotho's Spindle is first and foremost a fiber arts business that prides itself in providing unique and quality yarn for our customers. Clotho's Spindle is owned and operated by Addison and Susan Nishijima who both reside in the lovely state of Oregon.

WHO WE ARE

Addison does the majority of fiber dyeing at Clotho's Spindle. Besides dyeing, Addison spends their time studying to be a web designer and front end developer at Epicodus in Portland, Oregon.

Susan is what we like to call a “fiber junkie” and does many fiber crafts, including dyeing. She has worked in the fabric retail industry for many years. Sue teaches various crafts at a fabric/craft retail store.
